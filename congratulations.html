Congratulations!

You have successfully submitted an account application!

After Applying:

  • Please allow 2-3 days to allow us to accept or deny your application.
    • If we take longer than that, check your app status on this page.
      Your app may have been accepted or denied without you
      getting a message.
    • Then, please ask us to check your app in help chat (#site17).

When You Get In:

  • Once you've joined the site, please introduce yourself in one of this month's introduction threads. (Writer/reader/explorer, you choose!)
  • You can find the introduction threads at this link. Please do not make a new thread for your intro. Thank you!
  • Check out the Seminars Hub for introductory Welcome to the Wiki seminars!
  • If you're interested in the Foundation Universe but want to do something in a non-restrictive format, you can check out the Wanderers Library. It's the only official spin-off site of the SCP Wiki. The site premise is framed around the home base of the Serpent's Hand, the Wanderer's Library, a trove of every story ever written in every reality. It houses stories about the Hand and its members, as well as the many stories contained within the library itself.

If you got denied:

Reasons:
Did you get the passcode right?
Did you include your age?
Did you put ONLY your current age and the passcode and NOTHING ELSE like you're supposed to?
Does your username and profile pic comply with the rules?

If the answer to all of those things is yes, it was probably a glitch.
If the answer is no, fix it before reapplying.

What to do:

You'll need to go here, and delete the old application before reapplying.

You need to click on the denied application before the option to delete it will appear.

If you are still confused, contact staff.

Confused?

If you have any questions, you can ask staff in our help chat (#site17).

If you can't get your application accepted, make sure you're typing the right passcode (don't guess). You can ask for help in #site17.

On a phone and can't sign in? Please make sure you're using the URL http://scp-wiki.wikidot.com/system:join and not http://scpwiki.com/system:join — the second link may not allow you to sign in on mobile!
